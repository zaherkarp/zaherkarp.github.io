The Shape of a HEDIS Measure Pipeline

B[Eligible Population] 
C[Claims/Encounters] --> B
B --> D[Numerator Events]
B --> E[Exclusions]
D --> F[Rate Calculation]
E --> F
G[Supplemental Data] --> F

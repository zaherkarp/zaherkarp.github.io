## Tips for Writing:

1. **Title**: Make it clear and compelling
2. **Description**: This appears in the blog list and social previews
3. **PublishDate**:
   - Use today's date for immediate publishing
   - Use a past date for backdating migrated content
4. **Draft**:
   - `draft: true` = Post won't appear on the site
   - `draft: false` or omit = Post is published
   - Use the "Publish Blog Post" GitHub Action to publish drafts
5. **Tags**: Help organize and categorize your posts

## Filename Guidelines:

- Use lowercase letters
- Separate words with hyphens
- Be descriptive but concise
- Example: `my-awesome-tutorial.md`
- Save to: `src/content/blog/your-filename.md`

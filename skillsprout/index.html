SkillSprout, Career Trajectory Explorer

Pick a starting occupation. SkillSprout compares its skill fingerprint against 1,016 O*NET occupations using IDF-weighted Jaccard matching and returns three buckets: Ready Now (strong overlap, same or lower training zone), Trainable (mostly overlapping skills with specific gaps), and Long-Term Reskill (adjacent fields worth a longer runway). Runs entirely in your browser, no backend, no data leaves this page.
